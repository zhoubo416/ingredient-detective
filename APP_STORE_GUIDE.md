# 配料侦探 - App Store 上架指南

## 📱 应用基本信息

### 应用名称
- **中文**: 配料侦探
- **英文**: Ingredient Detective

### 应用描述（中文）
"配料侦探"是一款智能食品配料分析应用，通过AI技术帮助您快速识别食品配料表中的营养成分、加工程度和合规性。只需拍摄食品配料表照片，即可获得专业的营养分析和健康建议。

**主要功能：**
- 📸 智能拍照识别配料表
- 🔍 AI分析配料营养成分
- 📊 加工度评估和健康评分
- ⚖️ 合规性检测和特定宣称分析
- 📈 历史记录和趋势分析

### 应用描述（英文）
"Ingredient Detective" is an intelligent food ingredient analysis app that uses AI technology to help you quickly identify nutritional components, processing levels, and compliance in food ingredient lists. Simply take a photo of the food ingredient list to get professional nutritional analysis and health advice.

**Key Features:**
- 📸 Smart photo recognition of ingredient lists
- 🔍 AI analysis of nutritional components
- 📊 Processing level assessment and health scoring
- ⚖️ Compliance detection and specific claim analysis
- 📈 History records and trend analysis
